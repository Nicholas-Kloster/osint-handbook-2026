# Localised Search

> 9 tools — [osinthandbook.com/localised-search](https://www.osinthandbook.com/localised-search)

- [brightlocal](https://www.brightlocal.com/local-search-results-checker) — A localised SERP checker that lets you view Google or Google Maps search results from any location worldwide.
- [dentsu: Local Search Results Tool](https://technicalseo.com/tools/local-search) — Check Google or Bing local search results from any location, including options for desktop or mobile views, and city- or ZIP code-based queries.
- [GeoSearch: 'VPN' for Google Search](https://chromewebstore.google.com/detail/geosearch-vpn-for-google/lelookjlkhplmnfckjeecmpimklobeee) — Simulate Google searches from different locations to view localized search results.
- [Global Search Simulator](https://chromewebstore.google.com/detail/global-search-simulator-p/nlfdmoeliaddhkggbkcchaanhccdplhl) — Simulate Google searches from different countries and in different languages.
- [GoogleSwitch](https://chromewebstore.google.com/detail/googleswitch/gleogimfdeaapagejhfmlgkgenfiaoha) — Switch between different Google search domains to compare localized search results.
- [Impersonal.me](http://www.impersonal.me) — Simulates Google searches from over 70 countries and in 40+ languages.
- [SEO Search Simulator](https://chromewebstore.google.com/detail/seo-search-simulator-by-n/edfjfgjklednkencfhnokmkajbgfhpon?hl=en) — Simulate Google search queries from any location.
- [Valentin.app](https://valentin.app) — Provides an easy way to get localized Google search results.
- [VPN Mentor](https://www.vpnmentor.com/tools/search-from) — Allows you to change country in Google and perform a specific location search from any place or device. It also lets you use Google Search in a different language.
