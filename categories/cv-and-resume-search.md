# Cv And Resume Search

> 10 tools — [osinthandbook.com/cv-and-resume-search](https://www.osinthandbook.com/cv-and-resume-search)

- [Analyst Search Tools](https://analystresearchtools.com) — An extensive collection of OSINT tools and resources.
- [Bayt](https://www.bayt.com) — Connects job seekers with employment opportunities across the Middle East and North Africa.
- [CareerBuilder](https://hiring.careerbuilder.com) — Job posting and resume-search platform connecting employers with candidates.
- [Job Vertise Search](https://www.jobvertise.com/resumes/search) — Free platform providing jobs and resume database.
- [Linkedin](https://www.linkedin.com) — Professional networking site enabling individuals to build career-focused connections, share resumes, and discover job opportunities.
- [Mighty Recruiter](https://www.mightyrecruiter.com) — End-to-end recruiting solution offering free access to an extensive resume database.
- [Monster](https://hiring.monster.com) — Comprehensive recruitment platform offering resume search among other features.
- [Recruit’em](https://recruitin.net) — Tool that generates advanced Google search queries to find profiles on various professional and social networking sites.
- [Visualize.me](http://vizualize.me) — Creates visually appealing resumes using infographics and data visualisation.
- [XING](https://www.xing.com) — Career-oriented social network focused on German-speaking countries (D-A-CH), enabling professionals to connect, search jobs, join groups, and network locally.
