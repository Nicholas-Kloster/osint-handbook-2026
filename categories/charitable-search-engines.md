# Charitable Search Engines

> 12 tools — [osinthandbook.com/charitable-search-engines](https://www.osinthandbook.com/charitable-search-engines)

- [Blue Search](https://blue-search.org) — Blue directs search revenue to ocean restoration projects aiming to rebuild marine ecosystems.
- [Ecosia](https://www.ecosia.org) — Ecosia uses its profits to plant trees and fund climate action initiatives globally.
- [Ekoru](https://ekoru.org) — Ekoru is a search engine that helps remove plastic from oceans with each search.
- [gexsi](https://gexsi.com) — Gexsi is a purpose-driven search engine that funds social innovation projects worldwide.
- [Givero](https://www.givero.com) — Givero donates a portion of its search revenue to user-selected charities.
- [giveWater](https://www.givewater.com) — GiveWater directs search-generated profits to provide clean water to communities in need.
- [goodsearch](https://www.goodsearch.com) — Goodsearch is a Yahoo-powered search engine that donates 50% of its ad revenue to user-selected charities.
- [Lilo](https://www.lilo.org) — Lilo funds social and environmental projects through ad revenue generated by user searches.
- [Ocean Hero](https://oceanhero.today) — OceanHero recovers ocean-bound plastic bottles with every five searches performed.
- [OCG](https://ocg.org) — OCG uses search revenue to fund ocean cleanup and marine life rescue efforts.
- [Rapusia](https://rapusia.org) — Rapusia is a search engine that donates its profits to social and environmental projects.
- [youcare](https://youcare.world) — YouCare transforms searches into charitable donations while offsetting carbon emissions.
