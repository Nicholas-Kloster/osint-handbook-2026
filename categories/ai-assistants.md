# Ai Assistants

> 12 tools — [osinthandbook.com/ai-assistants](https://www.osinthandbook.com/ai-assistants)

- [Apertus](https://publicai.co) — An open-source language model developed in Switzerland.
- [Bagoodex](https://bagoodex.io) — An AI chatbot and search engine with a simple, clean interface.
- [Bearly](https://bearly.ai) — Secure private AI chat platform offering access to multiple major LLMs (OpenAI, Anthropic, Gemini, Grok) with features like smart agents, image editing, and encrypted collaboration.
- [ChatGPT](https://chatgpt.com/) — An LLM chatbot developed by OpenAI and released in late 2022.
- [Claude](https://claude.ai) — Anthropic’s advanced AI chatbot.
- [Deepseek](https://www.deepseek.com) — A Chinese AI company developing powerful open-source large language models.
- [Gemini](https://gemini.google.com/) — Google's AI assistant.
- [Grok](https://grok.com) — A free AI assistant designed by xAI.
- [HuggingChat](https://huggingface.co/chat/) — An open-source AI chatbot interface—an alternative to ChatGPT hosted by Hugging Face.
- [Lumo AI](https://lumo.proton.me/) — The zero-access encrypted AI assistant by Proton that does not track or record your conversations.
- [Meta AI](https://www.meta.ai) — An AI assistant and creative tool by Meta.
- [Microsoft Copilot](https://copilot.microsoft.com) — Microsoft's AI assistant.
