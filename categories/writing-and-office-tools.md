# Writing And Office Tools

> 14 tools — [osinthandbook.com/writing-and-office-tools](https://www.osinthandbook.com/writing-and-office-tools)

- [Apple iWork](https://www.apple.com/iwork/) — Cloud-based productivity suite including documents, spreadsheets, and presentations with rich templates, Apple Pencil support, and real-time collaboration across Apple devices and the web.
- [FreeOffice](https://www.freeoffice.com) — Fully free office suite featuring a word processor, spreadsheets, and presentation tools, compatible with Microsoft Office formats and available on Windows, Mac, and Linux.
- [GoogleDocs](https://docs.google.com) — Web-based collaborative word processor enabling real-time editing, revision history, and rich exporting options.
- [LibreOffice](https://www.libreoffice.org) — Free, open-source office productivity suite offering word processing, spreadsheets, presentations, drawing, databases, and formula editing with strong Microsoft Office compatibility.
- [Manuskript](http://www.theologeek.ch/manuskript/) — An open source tool that helps writers plan and organise a manuscript using methods like the snowflake technique, keeping track of characters, plot, events, and places in a structured way.
- [MS Office](https://products.office.com) — Comprehensive suite of cloud-based productivity applications like Word, Excel, PowerPoint, Outlook, Teams, OneDrive, with both premium subscription and one-time purchase options.
- [Ommwriter](https://ommwriter.com) — Minimalist, full-screen writing tool designed to shield users from distractions by providing a calming environment with optional background audio and keystroke sounds.
- [OmniOutliner](https://www.omnigroup.com/omnioutliner) — Outlining software optimised for macOS and iOS, enabling users to structure and view ideas with features like inline notes, drag-and-drop, row numbering, rich text, export options, and distraction-free writing.
- [OnlyOffice](https://www.onlyoffice.com) — Self‑hosted or cloud office suite combining document editing, spreadsheets, presentations and team workspace collaboration.
- [Plume Creator](https://plume-creator.eu/) — Scene-and-strand-based creative writing tool that lets you write in an organised, interactive environment, with chapter / scene structure, notes, and synopsis management.
- [Scrivener](https://www.literatureandlatte.com/scrivener.php) — Combines outlining and word-processing tools with a corkboard and binder interface to organise scenes, chapters, notes, research, and templates for various genres.
- [StoryToolz](https://storytoolz.com/) — Offers a collection of free writing-related utilities for authors, including story generators, word-count meters, readability analysis, title creators, cliché busters, and more.
- [UltraEdit](https://www.ultraedit.com/) — High-performance, configurable text and code editor with large-file handling, integrated AI assistance, Git plugins, scripting, and multi-platform support.
- [Ulysses](https://ulyssesapp.com) — Offers a focused, distraction-free writing environment on Mac, iPad, and iPhone with seamless sync, flexible export, and document management in one workflow.
