# Finding Prompts

> 6 tools — [osinthandbook.com/finding-prompts](https://www.osinthandbook.com/finding-prompts)

- [Prompt Base](https://promptbase.com) — A marketplace for buying and selling curated AI prompt templates.
- [PromptBox](https://www.promptbox.ai) — An AI prompt-management tool that helps you organise, store, test, and share prompts efficiently.
- [Promptist](https://huggingface.co/spaces/microsoft/Promptist) — An experimental generative AI assistant by Microsoft within Hugging Face Spaces for crafting, editing, and refining prompts.
- [PromptLayer](https://www.promptlayer.com) — A platform that logs, tracks, and analyzes AI prompt usage.
- [Prompts Chat](https://prompts.chat) — An extensive collection of AI prompts with tabs corresponding to different AI assistants / chatbots.
- [Public Prompts](https://publicprompts.art) — A free, open, community-driven collection of AI prompts, models, and resources for creative AI projects.
