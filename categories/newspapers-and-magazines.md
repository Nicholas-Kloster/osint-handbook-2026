# Newspapers And Magazines

> 16 tools — [osinthandbook.com/newspapers-and-magazines](https://www.osinthandbook.com/newspapers-and-magazines)

- [East view: Newspaper](https://www.eastview.com/resources/newspapers/) — Access to global newspaper databases and digital archives.
- [Google News Print Archive](https://news.google.com/newspapers) — Archive of scanned historic newspapers from Google News.
- [Library of Congress – US Newspapers](https://chroniclingamerica.loc.gov/search/titles/) — Library of Congress archive of historic American newspapers.
- [Newspaper Archive](https://newspaperarchive.com) — Digital newspaper archive with historical editions.
- [Newspaper Map](https://newspapermap.com) — Interactive map linking to newspapers worldwide by location.
- [Newspaperindex](https://www.newspaperindex.com/) — Collection of major newspapers categorised by country.
- [Newspapers.com](https://www.newspapers.com/) — Online archive of historical and contemporary newspapers.
- [NewsStand](https://www.newsstand.co.uk) — UK-focused digital newsstand providing access to a variety of publications.
- [OnlineNewspapers](https://onlinenewspapers.com) — Index of online newspapers by country and language.
- [Paperboy](http://www.thepaperboy.com) — Directory of online newspapers sorted by country and region.
- [Press Reader](http://www.pressreader.com) — Digital platform offering access to global newspaper editions.
- [Readly](https://us.readly.com) — Subscription service offering unlimited access to digital magazines and newspapers.
- [W3newspapers](https://www.w3newspapers.com) — Directory of world newspapers and news sites by region.
- [Wikipedia: List of Online Newspaper Archives](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Wikipedia:List_of_online_newspaper_archives) — List of online newspaper archives.
- [World Newspapers](https://www.world-newspapers.com/) — Directory of newspapers from around the world by region.
- [YUMPU](https://www.yumpu.com/news/en) — Digital newsstand hosting magazines and newspapers in multiple languages.
