# Browsing History

> 5 tools — [osinthandbook.com/browsing-history](https://www.osinthandbook.com/browsing-history)

- [Better History](https://chromewebstore.google.com/detail/better-history/egehpkpgpgooebopjihjmnpejnjafefi) — Offers powerful search, bulk export, custom deletion by day or hour, and domain blocking to enhance Chrome's history management.
- [Chrome History View](https://www.nirsoft.net/utils/chrome_history_view.html) — Displays Chrome's browsing history with details like URL, title, visit time, and allows exporting to various formats.
- [Firefox Better History](https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/firefox-better-history/) — Provides daily, weekly, and monthly views for browsing history, inspired by Vivaldi's interface.
- [History Plus](https://chromewebstore.google.com/detail/history-plus/kloodnjmhgicecceindgbfpjencnhajh) — Extends Chrome's history beyond 90 days with advanced search, auto-backup, import/export, and smart categorisation for easy management.
- [Improved History](https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/improved-history/) — Offers a stylish toolbar popup UI for Firefox's history with day-based navigation, search functionality, and quick deletion options.
