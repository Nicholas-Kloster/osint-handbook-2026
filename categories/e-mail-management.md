# E Mail Management

> 14 tools — [osinthandbook.com/e-mail-management](https://www.osinthandbook.com/e-mail-management)

- [ActiveInbox](https://www.activeinboxhq.com/) — Turns Gmail into a more productivity-focused tool, including follow-ups, templates, scheduling, and reminders within Gmail interface.
- [Batched Inbox](https://www.batchedinbox.com) — A Gmail assistant that delivers emails only at times you specify throughout the day, so you can avoid distractions and focus on what matters.\n\n
- [Boomerang for Gmail](https://www.boomeranggmail.com/) — A popular meeting scheduling and email management tool.
- [Cleanfox](https://www.cleanfox.io) — Lets you identify, block, or remove newsletters and unwanted subscriptions in bulk with minimal effort.
- [ClearContext](https://www.clearcontext.com/) — An e-mail workflow assistant that reminds you to follow up on un-answered emails, turns emails into tasks or appointments, snoozes messages out of your Inbox, and more.
- [Followupthen](https://www.followupthen.com) — Tool to schedule follow-up reminders via email (send or forward an email and have it come back at a later time).
- [Hiver](https://hiverhq.com/) — Tool for shared inboxes, collaborating on email, email assignment, and SLA tracking in teams.
- [Integrated gmail](https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/integrated-gmail/) — A Firefox add-on to integrate Gmail, Chat, and other Google Workspace functions into a more unified interface within the browser.
- [Mailstore](https://www.mailstore.com/) — Software to archive and backup emails locally: supports many email providers, stores searchable copies of messages, attachments, etc.
- [NudgeMail](https://nudgemail.com/) — A simple app that lets you send reminder emails by forwarding or writing a message to Nudgemail using time-based addresses (e.g. “tomorrow@nudgemail.com”).
- [Sanebox](https://www.sanebox.com) — Service that automatically sorts, prioritizes and declutters your inbox.
- [Sortd](https://www.sortd.com/) — Transforms Gmail’s inbox into a more visual task board / Kanban-style layout to manage emails as tasks.
- [Ugly Email](https://uglyemail.com) — Flags or warns when tracking pixels or similar privacy-invasive features are embedded in incoming emails.
- [Wisestamp](https://www.wisestamp.com) — Allows adding dynamic, customizsd signature blocks to your outgoing email.
