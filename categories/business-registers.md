# Business Registers

> 12 tools — [osinthandbook.com/business-registers](https://www.osinthandbook.com/business-registers)

- [AML CTF: List of Company Registers](https://aml-cft.net/library/company-registers) — A list of company registers around the world.\n
- [Business Reference Services](http://www.loc.gov/rr/business/company/directories.html) — Doing Company Research: A Resource Guide
- [Business Registers in EU Countries](https://e-justice.europa.eu/106/EN/business_registers_in_eu_countries) — A list of business registers in EU countries.\n
- [Company Research Resources by Country](http://www.rba.co.uk/sources/registers.htm) — A list of official company registers.
- [Knowledge Guide to International Company Registration](https://www.icaew.com/en/library/subject-gateways/business-management/company-administration/knowledge-guide-international-company-registration) — Company information sources by jurisdiction.
- [Molfar: Public and Business Registers](https://www.molfar.institute/en/analytics/registers) — A list of public and business registers from all across the world.
- [OCCRPID Catalogue of Research Databases](https://id.occrp.org/databases/) — A collection of public data sources.
- [Open Corporate Data](http://registries.opencorporates.com) — The Open Company Data Index shows how countries around the world allow company registration information to be publicly accessible.
- [Overseas Company Registers](https://www.gov.uk/government/publications/overseas-registries/overseas-registries) — A comprehensive list of company registries located around the world.
- [The Business Disputes Register: Company Registries by Country](https://www.disputesregister.org/advice/company-registries-by-country) — Company registers by country.
- [Wikipedia: National Company Registers](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/List_of_company_registers) — A list of official business registers.
- [Worldwide OSINT Tools Map](https://cybdetective.com/osintmap) — A map showing OSINT resources, including business registers, across the world.
