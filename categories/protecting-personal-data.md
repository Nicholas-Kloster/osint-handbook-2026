# Protecting Personal Data

> 12 tools — [osinthandbook.com/protecting-personal-data](https://www.osinthandbook.com/protecting-personal-data)

- [Abine](https://www.abine.com) — The online privacy company offering a set of privacy protection tools.
- [Big-Ass-Data-Broker-Opt-Out-List](https://github.com/yaelwrites/Big-Ass-Data-Broker-Opt-Out-List) — An actively maintained data broker opt out list.
- [Cloaked](https://www.cloaked.com/) — Email alias and privacy protection service.
- [IronVest](https://ironvest.com/app/blur-sign-up/) — Privacy-focused digital identity and email masking tool.
- [Mine](https://www.saymine.com/) — Tool to request and manage personal data from companies.
- [My Sudo](https://mysudo.com) — All-in-one privacy app offering virtual phone numbers, email, browsing, and payment profiles.
- [MyPermissions](https://mypermissions.com) — Manage and revoke app permissions and data access.
- [NoMoRobo](https://nomorobo.com/) — Robocall blocking and spam call protection service.
- [Prism Break](https://prism-break.org) — Directory of privacy-respecting software alternatives.
- [Sudo App](https://sudoapp.com) — All-in-one privacy platform.
- [ToSDR](https://tosdr.org) — Analysis and rating of website terms of service.
- [Yorba](https://yorba.co) — Delete unwanted accounts, emails, and subscriptions.
