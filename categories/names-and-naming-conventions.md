# Names And Naming Conventions

> 8 tools — [osinthandbook.com/names-and-naming-conventions](https://www.osinthandbook.com/names-and-naming-conventions)

- [A Guide to Names and Naming Practices](https://www.fbiic.gov/public/2008/nov/Naming_practice_guide_UK_2006.pdf) — A document detailing naming standards and guidelines used in UK criminal intelligence reporting.
- [Behind The Name](https://www.behindthename.com) — Provides detailed etymologies, meanings, and historical usage of given names from various cultures and time periods.
- [Forebears](https://forebears.io/) — Offers a global database of surnames and forenames, providing information on name meanings, distributions, and genealogical resources.
- [Indian Naming Conventions](http://learningindia.in/indian-naming-conventions/) — Informative guide explaining Indian naming conventions, structures, and cultural contexts.
- [Law Enforcement Guide to International Names](https://info.publicintelligence.net/ROCICInternationalNames.pdf) — Global law enforcement reference PDF listing international naming conventions and practices.
- [Nameberry](https://nameberry.com) — Presents an extensive database of baby names with origins, meanings, and trend analyses.
- [NAMINT](https://seintpl.github.io/NAMINT/) — Generates search queries based on inputted names and usernames to assist with open-source intelligence investigations.
- [Russian Names](https://www.russlandjournal.de/russisch-lernen/russische-namen) — Resource for learning Russian personal names, their meanings, forms, and naming patterns.
