# Document Checking

> 6 tools — [osinthandbook.com/document-checking](https://www.osinthandbook.com/document-checking)

- [Henley Passport Index](https://www.henleyglobal.com/passport-index) — Offers global rankings of passports based on the number of destinations their holders can access without a prior visa.
- [Keesing International ID Documents](https://www.keesingtechnologies.com/reference-database/id-documents) — Features a comprehensive database of ID documents used worldwide, aiding in verification and fraud detection for professionals.
- [Nasjonalt ID-senter](https://www.nidsenter.no) — Serves as Norway’s national ID centre providing expertise, training, and resources related to identity documents and fraud prevention.
- [Passport Index](https://www.passportindex.org) — Displays passport rankings, visa requirements, and travel freedom information with interactive comparisons and visual tools.
- [Passport Stamp](https://www.wikiwand.com/en/articles/Passport_stamp) — Provides an enhanced interface for browsing detailed articles about passport stamps, their designs, and their use across different countries.
- [PRADO](https://www.consilium.europa.eu/prado/EN/search-by-document-country.html) — Allows users to search for and view official travel and identity documents issued by EU member states and other countries.
