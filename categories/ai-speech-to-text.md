# Ai Speech To Text

> 12 tools — [osinthandbook.com/ai-speech-to-text](https://www.osinthandbook.com/ai-speech-to-text)

- [Assembly AI](https://www.assemblyai.com) — Developer-friendly API platform offering speech-to-text transcription and advanced speech understanding for building voice-based applications and analytics pipelines.
- [Bearly](https://bearly.ai) — Secure private AI chat platform offering access to multiple major LLMs (OpenAI, Anthropic, Gemini, Grok) with features like smart agents, image editing, and encrypted collaboration.
- [Contentfries](https://www.contentfries.com) — An AI-powered content creation and repurposing platform that specialises in short-form video clips.
- [Fireflies](https://fireflies.ai) — An AI meeting assistant that automatically joins, records, transcribes, and summarises Zoom, Google Meet, and Teams meetings—with integrations into tools like Slack and Notion.
- [FreeSubtitles AI](https://freesubtitles.ai) — A free AI transcription and translation tool, allowing users to upload audio / video for fast automatic subtitles and translations.
- [Heygen](https://www.heygen.com) — An AI video-generation platform for creating high-quality, multilingual videos with lifelike avatars, voice cloning, subtitles, and more.\n
- [Noty.ai](https://noty.ai) — A Meeting AI assistant that transcribes, summarises, and converts discussions into actionable to-dos to boost productivity.
- [Otter AI](https://otter.ai) — A meeting AI assistant that provides live transcription, automated summaries, speaker recognition, action items, and conversational search from recorded discussions.
- [Streamlabs Podcast Editor](https://streamlabs.com/podcast-editor/features/video-to-text) — Online video to text converter.
- [Supertranslate AI](https://www.supertranslate.ai) — AI-powered transcription and translation tool.
- [Whisper](https://github.com/openai/whisper) — Open-source general-purpose speech recognition model.
- [Whisper Memos](https://whispermemos.com) — iOS app that transcribes voice memos and emails.
