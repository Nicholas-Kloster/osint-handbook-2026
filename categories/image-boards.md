# Image Boards

> 9 tools — [osinthandbook.com/image-boards](https://www.osinthandbook.com/image-boards)

- [4Archive](https://4archive.org) — An archive site for storing and accessing past threads from imageboards.
- [4CAT](https://github.com/digitalmethodsinitiative/4cat) — The 4CAT Capture and Analysis Toolkit provides modular data capture and analysis for a variety of social media platforms.
- [4chan Search](https://boards.4chan.org/search) — Search portal to look through threads/posts on various 4chan boards.
- [4ChanSearch](https://4chansearch.com) — Search engine for 4chan and its archive sites.
- [4plebs](https://4plebs.org/) — A site archiving content from selected 4chan boards.
- [8chan/8kun QResearch Board Search](https://qresear.ch) — Search engine covering #QResearch content across 8chan / 8kun and related boards.
- [All Chans](https://allchans.org/) — A directory of imageboards.
- [Endchan](https://endchan.net) — A popular, anonymous imageboard platform.
- [Find 4Chan](https://find.4chan.org) — Tool to search for posts on 4chan.
