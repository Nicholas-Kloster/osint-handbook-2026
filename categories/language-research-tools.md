# Language Research Tools

> 3 tools — [osinthandbook.com/language-research-tools](https://www.osinthandbook.com/language-research-tools)

- [Ethnologue](https://www.ethnologue.com) — Extensive global language database and reference, including language profiles, speaker numbers, and classifications.
- [Languages Spoken in Each Country of the World](https://www.infoplease.com/world/countries/languages-spoken-in-each-country-of-the-world) — Comprehensive listing of languages spoken in every country worldwide, with statistics and data.
- [Open Language Archive Community (OLAC)](http://olac.ldc.upenn.edu) — Online catalog of language resources, focusing on linguistic datasets, archives, and metadata from various institutions.
