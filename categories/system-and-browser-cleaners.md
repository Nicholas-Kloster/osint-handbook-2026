# System And Browser Cleaners

> 15 tools — [osinthandbook.com/system-and-browser-cleaners](https://www.osinthandbook.com/system-and-browser-cleaners)

- [BleachBit](https://www.bleachbit.org) — Free, open-source disk space cleaner and privacy manager to quickly delete junk files and maintain confidentiality.
- [CCEnhancer](https://singularlabs.com/software/ccenhancer) — Lightweight add-on for CCleaner that injects over 1,000 additional cleaning rules to enhance its scope.
- [CCleaner](https://www.piriform.com/ccleaner) — Cleaner for PC helps you clean up your Windows computer, optimise performance, and maintain your online privacy.
- [Cleanersoft](https://www.cleanersoft.com) — A safe and easy-to-use privacy protection tool, that allows you to erase common Internet and computing tracks, including browser cache, cookies, visited websites, typed URLs, recent documents, index.dat files and the tracks of up to 100 popular applications.
- [CleanMyMac](https://macpaw.com/cleanmymac) — All-in-one macOS cleaner that removes junk, malware, duplicates, and performance issues.
- [Click&Clean](https://chromewebstore.google.com/detail/clickclean/ghgabhipcejejjmhhchfonmamedcbeod) — Deletes typed URLs, cache, cookies, your download and browsing history instantly, with just  one click on Click&Clean button.
- [Cookie AutoDelete](https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/cookie-autodelete/?src=search) — Automatically deletes cookies when a tab is closed.
- [DBAN](https://dban.org) — Open-source bootable tool to permanently wipe data from HDDs (not SSDs), removing viruses and spyware.
- [Eraser](https://eraser.heidi.ie/) — Secure file erasure tool for Windows that permanently removes sensitive files beyond recovery.
- [History Cleaner](https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/history-cleaner/?src=collection) — Deletes browsing history older than a specified number of days.
- [HotCleaner](https://www.hotcleaner.com) — Security-focused site offering tips to enhance privacy and system speed through safer browsing and cleaning practices.
- [MacBooster](https://www.macbooster.net) — Mac cleanup and optimisation suite that frees up disk space, secures against threats, and boosts performance.
- [Privazer](https://privazer.com) — Free PC cleaner and privacy tool.
- [Revo Uninstaller](https://www.revouninstaller.com) — Windows uninstaller that thoroughly removes applications plus residual files and registry entries.
- [WiseCleaner](https://www.wisecleaner.com) — Comprehensive utility suite offering registry cleaners, system cleaners, privacy erasers, and other PC maintenance tools.
