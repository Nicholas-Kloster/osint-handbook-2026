# Working With Citations

> 13 tools — [osinthandbook.com/working-with-citations](https://www.osinthandbook.com/working-with-citations)

- [Bibcitation](https://www.bibcitation.com) — Free tool to create bibliographies and works cited in MLA, APA, Chicago, etc., with export options.
- [Bibliography](https://www.bibliography.com) — Fast, free MLA, APA & Chicago citation generator with export and collaboration options.
- [Bibme](https://www.bibme.org/) — A free bibliography and citation generator supporting APA, MLA, Chicago, and more.
- [Citation Generator](https://www.citethisforme.com/us/citation-generator) — Automatically formats bibliographies in APA, MLA, Chicago (7th–9th ed.), plus other styles.
- [Citation Machine](https://www.citationmachine.net/) — Comprehensive citation generator for APA, MLA, Chicago, and Harvard with instant bibliography export.
- [Citationsy*](https://citationsy.com) — Online citation and reference manager for organising and generating formatted citations.
- [Cite This For Me](https://www.citethisforme.com/) — Online citation manager and generator supporting multiple styles.
- [Citefast](https://www.citefast.com) — APA, MLA and Chicago citation generator with autofill / manual modes and many source types.
- [CustomWritings.com: Bibliography Generator](https://www.customwritings.com/citation_generator.html) — Free citation-generator tool supporting APA, MLA, Chicago, Harvard, etc.
- [EasyBib](https://www.easybib.com) — EasyBib free bibliography generator supporting MLA, APA, Chicago, and Harvard with export and writing assistance tools.
- [KnightCite](https://www.calvin.edu/library/knightcite/) — Free citation generator by Calvin University’s Hekman Library, using APA, MLA, and Chicago.
- [MyBib](https://www.mybib.com) — A free APA, MLA, and Chicago citation generator.
- [MyBib: Citation](https://www.mybib.com/#/projects/49ZAam/citations) — Free bibliography and citation generator that auto-creates citations from titles, URLs or ISBNs in many styles.
