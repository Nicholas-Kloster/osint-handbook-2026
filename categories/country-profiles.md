# Country Profiles

> 14 tools — [osinthandbook.com/country-profiles](https://www.osinthandbook.com/country-profiles)

- [Amnesty International: Countries](https://www.amnesty.org/en/countries/) — Offers human rights reports and updates for individual countries around the world.
- [BBC News Country Profile](http://news.bbc.co.uk/2/hi/country_profiles/default.stm) — Delivers concise country profiles with key facts, figures, and historical background.
- [CIA World Factbook](https://www.cia.gov/the-world-factbook/) — Features comprehensive background information on the history, people, government, economy, and geography of countries.
- [Countries and Areas](https://www.state.gov/countries-and-areas-list/) — Contains official U.S. government information on foreign countries and regions.
- [Country Policy and Information Notes](https://www.gov.uk/government/collections/country-policy-and-information-notes) — Provides policy and information notes used to assess asylum and protection claims in the UK.
- [Country Studies](http://countrystudies.us) — Provides in-depth country studies published by the U.S. Library of Congress.
- [ECOI Country Profiles](https://www.ecoi.net/) — Aggregates reports and documents from various reliable sources about countries' human rights and political situations.
- [Global Edge](https://globaledge.msu.edu) — Gives detailed data and analysis on countries' economies, industries, and global competitiveness.
- [IMUNA Country Profiles](https://www.imuna.org/resources/country-profiles) — Offers model United Nations country profiles with political, economic, and social insights.
- [Library of Congress Country Studies](https://www.loc.gov/collections/country-studies/about-this-collection) — Presents detailed historical, social, economic, and political information on countries worldwide.
- [Library of Congress Nations](http://www.loc.gov/law/help/guide/nations.php) — Contains legal research guides and links to laws and regulations by country.
- [Nations Online](https://www.nationsonline.org) — Provides general information about countries including geography, culture, politics, and travel facts.
- [UN Country Profiles](https://unstats.un.org/unsd/dnss/cp/searchcp.aspx) — Lists national statistical systems and provides access to official country profiles and related data.
- [UNCTAD Country Profile](http://unctadstat.unctad.org/CountryProfile/en-GB/index.html) — Supplies statistical country profiles focusing on trade, economy, and development indicators.
