# Customising New Tab

> 14 tools — [osinthandbook.com/customising-new-tab](https://www.osinthandbook.com/customising-new-tab)

- [Bookmark Dial](https://addons.mozilla.org/en-GB/firefox/addon/bookmark-dial/) — Displays the contents of a bookmark folder including a thumbnail every time you open a new tab.
- [Custom New Tabs](https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/custom-new-tabs/) — Allows users to set custom URLs for new tabs, providing flexibility in choosing the content displayed upon opening a new tab.
- [Group Speed Dial](https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/groupspeeddial/) — Enables the creation of speed dial groups in new tabs, offering organized access to frequently visited websites.
- [Humble New Tab](https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/humble-new-tab/) — A lightweight and customizable new tab page displaying bookmarks, most visited sites, and weather in a clean layout.
- [Infinite Dashboard](https://chrome.google.com/webstore/detail/infinite-dashboard-new-ta/meffljleomgifbbcffejnmhjagncfpbd) — Provides a customizable new tab experience with productivity tools, widgets, and a sleek interface.
- [Live Start Page LST](https://chrome.google.com/webstore/detail/live-start-page-living-wa/ocggccaacacpienfcgmgcihoombokbbj?utm_source=site&utm_medium=install) — Enhances your new tab with live wallpapers, a clock, and customizable widgets for a dynamic browsing experience.
- [Momentum](https://chrome.google.com/webstore/detail/momentum/laookkfknpbbblfpciffpaejjkokdgca?hl=en) — Offers a serene new tab with daily inspirational quotes, to-do lists, and weather updates to boost focus and motivation.
- [New Tab Draft](https://chrome.google.com/webstore/detail/new-tab-draft/nmfjkeiebceinkbggliapgfdjphocpdh) — Offers a minimalist new tab page for quick note-taking and calculations, storing data locally for enhanced privacy.
- [New Tab To Do List](https://chrome.google.com/webstore/detail/new-tab-todo-list-checkli/hdkbnhcgfcokjhlfiicbphafdnipnhjf) — Replaces your new tab with a dashboard featuring a to-do list, note-taking, and more.
- [Night Tab](https://chrome.google.com/webstore/detail/nighttab/hdpcadigjkbcpnlcpbcohpafiaefanki) — A privacy-friendly new tab extension offering customizable layouts, themes, and bookmark organization without data collection.
- [Searf](https://www.searf.com) — A customizable homepage that allows users to select and organize their favorite websites for quick access.
- [Speed Dial New Tab Page](https://chrome.google.com/webstore/detail/speed-dial-fvd-new-tab-pa/llaficoajjainaijghjlofdfmbjpebpa) — Provides a 3D visual bookmark manager with grouping, synchronization, and customizable backgrounds for your new tab.
- [Start.me](https://chrome.google.com/webstore/detail/new-tab-page-by-startme/cfmnkhhioonhiehehedmnjibmampjiab) — Turns your new tab into a personalized dashboard with widgets, bookmarks, and RSS feeds for streamlined productivity.
- [Tabliss](https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/tabliss/) — Transforms your new tab into a visually appealing page with customizable backgrounds and widgets.
