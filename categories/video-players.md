# Video Players

> 9 tools — [osinthandbook.com/video-players](https://www.osinthandbook.com/video-players)

- [5kPlayer](https://www.5kplayer.com) — A free (MKV) UHD video player, music player, AirPlay and DLNA enabled media streamer and online downloader.
- [Cisdem Video Player](https://www.cisdem.com/video-player-mac.html) — Mac and Windows video player supporting almost all video and audio formats.
- [DivX](https://www.divx.com) — Media player focused on high-quality video playback and DivX, AVI, MKV support.
- [GOM Player](https://www.gomlab.com/gomplayer-media-player/) — Media player with advanced features like subtitle sync and 360° video support.
- [KMPlayer](http://www.kmplayer.com) — Versatile multimedia player with wide format support and customisation options.
- [PotPlayer](http://potplayer.daum.net) — Windows multimedia player known for high performance and extensive format support.
- [Quicktime](https://support.apple.com/quicktime) — Official Apple support page for QuickTime, the built-in media player for macOS.
- [SMPlayer](https://www.smplayer.info) — Free media player with built-in codecs and YouTube support, based on MPlayer.
- [VLC](https://www.videolan.org/vlc/index.html) — A free and open source cross-platform multimedia player and framework that plays most multimedia files.
