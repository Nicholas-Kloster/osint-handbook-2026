# Researching Leaks

> 15 tools — [osinthandbook.com/researching-leaks](https://www.osinthandbook.com/researching-leaks)

- [Aleph - OCCRP Data: Investigative Dashboard Search](https://github.com/alephdata/aleph) — Data indexing platform for managing and investigating large sets of leaked and public records.
- [BalkanLeaks](https://balkanleaks.eu) — Platform focused on political and corporate accountability in the Balkans.
- [Cryptome](https://cryptome.org) — Archives and shares documents related to national security, surveillance, and intelligence activities.
- [DDOSECRETS](https://ddosecrets.com) — Publishes leaked and hacked data in the public interest, often focusing on government and corporate transparency.
- [GLOBALLEAKS](https://www.globaleaks.org) — Enables secure, anonymous submission of documents to journalists and other watchdog organisations.
- [Leak Scraper](https://github.com/Acceis/leakScraper/wiki/leakScraper) — A tool for searching leaked data datasets.
- [Library of Leaks](https://search.libraryofleaks.org) — Searchable archive of leaked documents and data breaches contributed by transparency activists.
- [OCCRP Aleph](https://data.occrp.org/datasets) — Provides access to investigative datasets used by journalists to expose corruption and organised crime.
- [Offshore Alert](https://www.offshorealert.com) — Offers news, intelligence, and research on high-value financial crimes and related investigations.
- [Offshore Leak Database](https://offshoreleaks.icij.org) — Database exposing offshore companies and the people behind them, based on leaked financial documents.\nBusiness Research::Company Search Tools
- [Organised Crime and Corruption Reporting Project (OCCRP)](https://www.occrp.org) — Investigative journalism platform focusing on organised crime and corruption worldwide.
- [Public Library of US Diplomacy](https://search.wikileaks.org/plusd/) — Allows users to search through diplomatic cables released as part of the WikiLeaks PlusD project.
- [Wikileaks](https://wikileaks.org) — Publishes classified, censored, or otherwise restricted materials of political, ethical, and historical significance.
- [Wikileaks Advanced Search](https://search.wikileaks.org/advanced) — Advanced search interface for accessing the full archive of WikiLeaks-released documents.
- [WikiSpooks](https://wikispooks.com) — Hosts user-generated content focused on deep politics, alternative theories, and covert operations.
