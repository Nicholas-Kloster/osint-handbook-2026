# Ads Tracking And Script Blockers

> 20 tools — [osinthandbook.com/ads-tracking-and-script-blockers](https://www.osinthandbook.com/ads-tracking-and-script-blockers)

- [AdblockPlus](https://adblockplus.org) — A popular adblocker that blocks ads, third-party trackers, and more.
- [Bitdefender Traffic Light](https://www.bitdefender.co.uk/solutions/trafficlight.html) — Free cross-browser add-on from Bitdefender that scans, filters, and blocks malicious content, phishing, trackers, and unsafe links.
- [Blokada](https://blokada.org/) — A popular suite of privacy apps for Android and iOS, including parental control, an ad blocker, and a VPN.
- [Decentraleyes](centralised) — A Firefox add-on that protects you against tracking through \free\"
- [Disconnect](https://disconnect.me/disconnect) — A set of tools for various platforms that block invisible trackers, speed up browsing, and protect against online privacy threats.
- [Disconnect from Facebook](https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/facebook-disconnect-ii/?src=collection) — An efficient firewall to prevent Facebook from tracking the webpages you go to by blocking all Facebook related requests sent from all third-party websites.
- [Do Not Track Me Google](https://chrome.google.com/webstore/detail/dont-track-me-google/gdbofhhdmcladcmmfjolgndfkpobecpg?hl=en) — A Chrome extension that prevents Google tracking via search and other services.
- [DuckDuckGo Privacy Essentials](https://chrome.google.com/webstore/detail/duckduckgo-privacy-essent/bkdgflcldnnnapblkhphbgpggdiikppg) — A Chrome extension by DuckDuckGo to block trackers and protect user's searches.
- [Facebook Container](https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/facebook-container/?src=collection) — A Firefox extension that allows Facebook content to run in a separate container to limit tracking between sites.
- [Firefox Multi-Account Containers](https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/multi-account-containers/?src=collection) — A Firefox extension that helps isolate different kinds of browsing in separate containers to segregate cookies and prevent cross-site tracking.
- [Forget Me Not](https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/forget_me_not/?src=featured) — A Firefox extension that makes the browser forget website data (like cookies, local storage, etc.), except for the data you want to keep by adding domains to a whitelist, graylist, blacklist, or redlist.
- [Ghostery](https://www.ghostery.com) — An extension available for different browsers that blocks ads and trackers, and protects users from malicious content.
- [Kimetrak](https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/kimetrak/?src=search) — A Firefox extension allowing you to see what third-party domains are loaded from websites you visit.
- [Link Cleaner](https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/link-cleaner/) — A Firefox extension that cleans up links to remove tracking parameters.
- [Nixory](http://nixory.sourceforge.net) — A free and open source antispyware tool written in Python / PyGTK aimed at removing malicious tracking cookies from your browser.
- [No Script](https://noscript.net) — A free browser extension that blocks malicious scripts and allows JavaScript only from trusted sites.
- [Privacy Badger](https://www.eff.org/privacybadger) — A free browser extension made by the leading digital rights nonprofit EFF to stop companies from spying on you online.
- [The Markup: Blacklight](https://themarkup.org/series/blacklight) — A real-time website privacy inspector. Enter the address of any website, and Blacklight will scan it and reveal the specific user-tracking technologies on the site.
- [Trackography](https://trackography.org) — A Tactical Tech project which aims to increase transparency about the online data industry by illustrating who tracks us when we browse the internet.
- [uBlock Origin](https://github.com/gorhill/uBlock) — A popular content blocker with a number of advanced features.
